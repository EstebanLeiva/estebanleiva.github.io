I'm an undergraduate senior at CSE, BUET. From 2022 to 2023, I have done research in collaboration with Augmented Design Lab under Jim Whitehead, publishing in top intelligent vehicles conferences such as IV 2023 and ITSC 2023.

My current work focuses on Generative Flow Networks and other discrete generative methods. I like agents that generalize well and adapt fast. My ongoing undergraduate thesis, under Dr. Mohammad Saifur Rahman , focuses on sequential antibody design, exploring better ways to sample antibody sequences in a conditional setting. Besides, my other work explores fast reward distribution adaptation for diverse MDPs in the GFlowNets framework at CogAI4Sci Lab under the amazing Dianbo Liu and Ling Pan.

As a sixth-year Indian classical musician trainee under Asit Dey, I belong to the Indore gharana. I also have a first-class specialization in Nazrul Sangeet from Chhayanaut Music School. As a multi-intrumental vocalist, I play harmonium, guitar, and piano, and I love organizing musical perfomances. When I am not doing research and music, I practice calisthenics, play the latest Zelda titles, and keep up with the newest meme trends.

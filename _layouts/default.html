Esteban Leiva

I am a Master's student in the Department of Industrial Engineering at Universidad de Los Andes, where I am affiliated with the Center for Optimization and Applied Probability (COPA).

My current research focuses on applying optimization techniques to stochastic shortest path problems and reinforcement learning. I work under the supervision of Andrés L. Medaglia and Mauricio Junca.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the Universidad de Los Andes in 2025, where my undergraduate thesis studied Inverse Reinforcement Learning and Apprenticeship Learning through the lenses of Inverse Optimization under the direction of Mauricio Junca.
